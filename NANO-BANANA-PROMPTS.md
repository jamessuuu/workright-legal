# Nano Banana Image Prompts - WorkRight Legal

Complete set of standalone image prompts for the WorkRight Legal website.
Each prompt is fully self-contained with all context needed to generate the image independently.

Color reference for all images:
- Deep teal: #2D4A3E
- Warm gold/amber: #C8973E
- Cream/warm white: #FAF7F2
- Charcoal: #1C1C1C

## 1. Homepage Hero - Office Wide Strip

**Save as**: `public/images/generated/hero-office-wide.jpg`
**Aspect ratio**: 21:9
**Format**: JPEG

**Prompt**:
A wide panoramic interior photograph of a premium modern law office in Sydney, Australia. The shot captures a long corridor with polished concrete floors and warm timber wall panels. On both sides of the corridor are glass-walled meeting rooms with black steel frames. Pendant lights hang from the ceiling at regular intervals, casting warm pools of light. Through floor-to-ceiling windows at the far end, there is a soft, out-of-focus glimpse of a city skyline with warm afternoon sunlight streaming in. The space features a low credenza in natural oak wood with a white ceramic bowl and a small potted plant on top. The overall color palette is deep teal walls (#2D4A3E), warm timber (#C8973E gold-toned wood), cream upholstery, and charcoal steel accents. No people are present. The atmosphere is calm, confident, and premium. Shot at eye level with a wide-angle lens, shallow depth of field blurring the far end. Editorial architectural photography style, natural lighting, no flash. The image should feel like walking into a law firm that takes your case seriously. Ultrawide 21:9 crop ratio.
