## About Priya Sharma

Priya Sharma brings a unique perspective to employment law through her dual background in law and psychology. She joined WorkRight Legal in 2022 after working in community legal centres where she advised vulnerable workers on their employment rights. Her understanding of workplace dynamics and the psychological impact of workplace misconduct informs her empathetic yet strategic approach to client representation.

Priya has a strong track record in workplace bullying stop orders, discrimination complaints to the Australian Human Rights Commission, and employment contract review. She is particularly effective at helping clients navigate emotionally difficult situations while building strong legal cases.

## Areas of Expertise

Priya specialises in workplace bullying and harassment, employment contract review and negotiation, workplace discrimination (including sex, race, disability, and age discrimination), and redundancy and severance disputes. Her psychology background gives her particular insight into the impact of workplace conduct and helps her effectively communicate client experiences to tribunals and courts.

## Approach

Priya believes in a client-centred approach that prioritises both legal outcomes and personal wellbeing. She takes time to understand each client's full situation, explains the legal process in plain language, and ensures clients feel supported at every step. Her combination of legal rigour and genuine empathy has earned her a strong reputation among clients and colleagues alike.
