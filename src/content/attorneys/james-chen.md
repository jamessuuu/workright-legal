## About James Chen

James Chen joined WorkRight Legal in 2019, bringing extensive experience in unfair dismissal and workplace discrimination matters. He has represented clients before the Fair Work Commission, the Federal Circuit and Family Court, and the NSW Industrial Relations Commission. His strategic approach and thorough case preparation have contributed to favourable outcomes in hundreds of employment disputes.

Before joining WorkRight Legal, James worked at a mid-tier commercial firm where he developed a strong foundation in employment law and litigation. His transition to specialist employment practice reflects his commitment to representing employees in workplace disputes.

## Areas of Expertise

James specialises in unfair dismissal claims, workplace discrimination and harassment, adverse action and general protections matters, and employment contract disputes. He has particular expertise in complex multi-party proceedings and cases involving systemic workplace issues.

## Approach

James takes a methodical, evidence-driven approach to every matter. He believes in being upfront with clients about the strengths and risks of their case, and works collaboratively to develop a strategy that achieves the best possible result. His clients consistently praise his clear communication and responsiveness throughout the legal process.
