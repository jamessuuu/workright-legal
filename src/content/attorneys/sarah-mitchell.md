## About Sarah Mitchell

Sarah Mitchell founded WorkRight Legal after dedicating her entire legal career to protecting employee rights across New South Wales. With over 15 years of employment law experience, she has successfully represented hundreds of clients in unfair dismissal claims, discrimination matters, and complex employment disputes before the Fair Work Commission and Federal Court.

Sarah's deep expertise in Australian workplace law, combined with her genuine passion for justice, means every client receives strategic, compassionate representation. She is known for her meticulous preparation, her ability to simplify complex legal issues, and her relentless advocacy for workers' rights.

## Areas of Expertise

Sarah specialises in unfair dismissal claims, workplace discrimination, general protections matters, employment contract negotiation and disputes, workplace bullying, and redundancy and severance negotiations. Her specialist focus means she stays at the forefront of employment law developments and brings insights from hundreds of cases to every new matter.

## Approach

Sarah believes that every employee deserves to be treated with dignity and fairness at work. Her approach combines thorough legal analysis with practical, strategic advice - always keeping the client's best interests and wellbeing at the centre of every decision.
