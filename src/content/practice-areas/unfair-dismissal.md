## Sydney's Specialist Unfair Dismissal Lawyers

Being dismissed from your job is one of the most stressful experiences you can face. If you believe your dismissal was harsh, unjust, or unreasonable, you may be entitled to compensation or reinstatement through the Fair Work Commission.

At WorkRight Legal, we exclusively practise employment law. This means every case we take on benefits from deep specialist knowledge and years of experience navigating the Fair Work system.

Our team has successfully represented clients dismissed in connection with workplace complaints or grievances, raising safety concerns, taking personal or parental leave, exercising workplace rights, alleged performance issues without proper process, misconduct allegations that were exaggerated or fabricated, sham redundancy situations, personality conflicts with management, discrimination based on age, gender, race, or disability, requesting flexible work arrangements, and union membership or activity.
