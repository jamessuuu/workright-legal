## Redundancy Pay Scale

Under the National Employment Standards, redundancy pay is calculated based on years of continuous service. Employees with 1 year of service receive 4 weeks' pay, increasing to 10 weeks at 5 years, 16 weeks at 9 years, and 12 weeks for 10 or more years. Your contract or enterprise agreement may provide more generous entitlements.
